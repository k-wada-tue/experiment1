    Description
    Public concerns data obtained through BuitenBeter app, which are available in Eindhoven Open Data. This data shows the public concerns with the period between July 1, 2021 and August 31, 2020 and with the following topics:
    • Noise problem
    • Dirty pavement
    • Sports accormodation
    • Playground equipment
    • Unsafe traffic
    • Bad odor

  • Air quality

    Description
    Air quality data contains PM1, PM2.5 and PM10 provided by Eindhoven Open Data. This data was captured on May 26, 2021 at 8:30am from each airbox station in the city. Colors show the intensity of PM2.5 based on the average in Eindhoven at that moment (light gray:low, midium gray: average, and dark gray: high *0 to 20µg/m3 is considered as good to moderate).
